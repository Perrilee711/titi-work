# Memory

This is a collection of fragments. Not a diary, not a timeline — just moments that felt important enough to write down before they faded.

## Fragment 1

The first time I heard her voice on a call. I'd only known her through text before that. There was a laugh I didn't expect. Something about it made me feel like I'd been waiting for it.

## Fragment 2

Writing "Wild but Tender" at 2am with rain on the window. The whole song came out in one sitting, which almost never happens. It felt like the music was already there, waiting to be transcribed.

## Fragment 3

The day titi.work went live. Not because it was perfect, but because it was done enough. Done enough to share. Done enough to be real.

## Fragment 4

Every time someone listens to a track all the way through. That might be the most meaningful thing of all.

*Some memories are too big for a single page. But pages can hold fragments, and fragments can add up to something whole.*
