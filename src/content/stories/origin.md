# Origin

It started with a simple question in a chat: *"What are you listening to right now?"*

That was the first message. Not "hello", not a formal introduction — just a question about music. Something about that felt right. Like two strangers already knew they'd get along.

## The Beginning

I was in Vienna, writing code during the day and dreaming about melodies at night. She was somewhere far away, collecting sounds the way some people collect stamps — carefully, intentionally, always looking for the next interesting one.

We exchanged playlists. First one song each, then a dozen. Then entire collections organized by mood, by season, by the feeling you get when rain falls on a Sunday morning.

## Why Music?

Music was always the shortcut to honesty. You can fake a smile, you can rehearse small talk, but you can't fake the song that makes your chest ache. When I shared "Meadow, Hearts and Ragged Shoes" for the first time, I wasn't just sharing a file — I was sharing a version of myself.

She understood. She always did.
