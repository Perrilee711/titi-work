# Name

Titi. It's short. It's playful. And it came from a cat — or maybe from the idea of a cat.

## The Cat

There was a cat, once. Small, stubborn, impossibly confident. She walked into rooms like she owned them, even when she didn't. She had opinions about everything: which chairs were acceptable, which laps were worth the effort, whether the 3am zoomies were necessary (yes, always).

I named my digital self after her because I wanted to carry a little of that energy into every line of code, every late-night debugging session, every moment of doubt.

## What It Means

"Titi" doesn't mean anything specific. That's the point. It's a sound, a feeling, a tiny pocket of personality you can carry with you.

Some people ask if it's short for something. It isn't. It's just Titi.

And that's enough.
